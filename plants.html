Peashooter

A basic attacking plant that shoots peas at zombies.

Sunflower

Produce regular sun to power your plant placements.

Cherry Bomb

Explodes in a 3x3 area, dealing massive damaege to all zombies.

Wall-nut

A defensive plant that blocks zombie progress with its tough shell.

Potato Mine

Buried mine that explodes on contact with zombies after arming.

Snow Pea

Shoot frozen peas that slow zombies.

Chomper

Devours zombies whole but takes time to chew.

Repeater

Shoots two peas at a time for double damage.

Puff Shroom

Short-range, free-shooting mushroom effective at night.

Sun Shroom

Produces small sun at first but grows to produce full sun over time.

Fume Shroom

Shoots fumes that penetrate screen doors and hit multiple zombies.

Grave Buster

Removes gravestones in night levels.

Hypno Shroom

Makes a zombie fight for you when eaten.

Scaredy Shroom

Long range shooter but hides when zombies get too close.

Ice Shroom

Freeze all zombies on the screen for a short time.

Doom Shroom

Causes a massive explosion in a large area, leaving craters.

Lily Pad

Allow plants to be placed on water.

Squash

Smashes zombies in its lane or adjacent lanes.

Threepeater

Shoots peas in three lanes at once.

Tangle Kelp

Pulls a zombie underwater, instantly defeating it.

Jalapeno

Destroys all zombies in one lane with fire.

Spikeweed

Damages zombies walking over it and pops tires.

Torchwood

Turns peas into fireballs, doubling their damage.

Tall-nut

A stronger version of wall-nut that blocks tall zombies.

Sea Shroom

Short range shooting mushroom usable in water.

Plantern

Lights up fog covered areas.

Cactus

Shoots spikes that pop balloon zombies.

Blover

Blows away fog and balloon zombies.

Split Pea

Shoots peas forward and backward.

Starfruit

Shoots stars in five directions.

Pumpkin

Protects plants by encasing them in a shell.

Kernel Pult

Throws kernels of corns at zombies, with a chance to throw butter to stun them.

Coffee Bean

Wakes up sleeping msuhrooms for use in the daytime.

Garlic

Redirects zombies to the other lanes when bitten.

Melon Pult

Lobs melon that deal heavy damage to groups of zombbies.

Winter Melon Pult

A stronger version of melon pult that slows zombies with the freezing effects.

Twin Sunflower

Produces double the sun ( upgrade the suflower ) .

Gatling Pea

Shoots four peas at a time ( upgrade to repeater ) .

Gold Magnet

Collects coins and diamonds for you ( upgrade to magnet shroom ) .

Spikerock

Stronger version of spikeweed that deals more damage and survives longer.

Cob Cannon

Launches massive corn cobs for heavy area damage ( requires two kernel pults ) .
